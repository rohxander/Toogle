Step Aside, my name's Redmi !

Redmi Note 4 in the town.

The game changer, Xiaomi has recently launched the Redmi Note 4 which is now available in India in three price variants.
Xiaomi has offered a very cool and slick full metal design in a low budget range. Redmi Note 4 comes in Gold, Silver and Black colors with a 2.5D Glass over the display making it absolutely gorgeous. The mobile is pretty handy and sleek with sharp edges.

Dimensions : 76mm x 151mm x 8.4mm
Its main feature is the gigantic 4100mAh battery. The battery can withstand a heavy usage for 18 hours which is very appreciable.
It has a 5 Mega Pixel front facing camera and a 13 Mega Pixel primary camera with CMOS sensor and BSI technology which in fact better than the 16 Mega Pixel camera of the Redmi Note 3. It has a dual tone flashlight and an Infrared remote sensor with which you control your day to day devices like TV or A/C very easily.
Talking about the display, Redmi Note 4 has a 5.5 inches full HD IPS display with a screen resolution of 1920x1080.The screen is quite sharp and clear since it has a pixel density of 401 pixels per inch. Unfortunately it does not have a Corning Gorilla Glass protection which very disappointing.
Note 4 is powered by a 64-bit Snapdragon 650 Octa Core processor with a clock speed of 2 GHz. Note 4 will also serve you with awesome gaming experience with its Adreno 510 Graphic Processor.

The company provides three variants of Xiaomi Redmi Note 4 with different RAM/Internal Storage space combination.
2GB/32GB Rs.9999
3GB/32GB Rs.10999
4GB/64GB Rs.12999
Redmi Note 4 is a Dual SIM phone with Hybrid SIM Slots which means that either you can use two SIM cards at a time or a single SIM card with an External Memory card in the hybrid slot.
The company has not brought much changes in the new Note 4 when compared to the Redmi Note 3. Looking at the specifications, Redmi Note 3 has a ‘Snapdragon 650 Hexa Core processor’ whereas the new Note 4 has a ‘Snapdragon 625 Octa Core processor’. The reason behind this change is to decrease battery consumption of the processor.
Overall, Redmi Note 4 will remain a wise option for the next 3 months atleast.
Other comparable smartphones in this price range are Xiaomi Redmi 4 Prime, Leeco 1s, Leeco 2, Zenfone 3 Max and Lenovo k6 Note .
