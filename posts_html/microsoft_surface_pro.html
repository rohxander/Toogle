Introducing to you Asus GL552 from the Republic Of Gaming series. It is a complete budget/High End gaming laptop and will cost you around $1000 or around Rs.90, 000.
Starting from the looks, gamers will love this laptop. It has a cool texture over its back panel and red theme keyboard and prints with red colored backlit keyboard. The laptop has four USB 3.0 ports, one UBS 3.1 type C port, Bluetooth 4.0 connectivity .It has a Full HD 15.6 inch IPS anti-glare display with 1920 x 1080 resolution which is pretty bright with nearly 300-nits brightness and 750:1 contrast ratio.

Back to the keyboard, you will find that the keyboard is just okay and not that satisfying if you are more into typing. The track pad feels smooth but lacks sensitivity when it comes to gentle and precise gesture swipes but you can adjust the sensitivity and cursor speed easily according to your needs.
The laptop is powered by a Skylake Core i7 6th Gen processor with a clock speed of 2.6 GHz and turbo boost upto 3.5GHz, paired with 16 GB of DDR4 RAM. The Graphic Processing Unit is a 2BG GDDR5 Nvidia GTX 960M Graphics. The laptop comes with a hybrid storage, with M.2 SATA 128 GB SSD and 2.5‚Äù 1 TB HDD.

Looking at the configuration the laptop is very powerful. It can easily run browsing and multimedia contents including 4K videos very easily.The Nvidia 960M graphics card can easily handle pretty high in games but we can't expect it to run most recent titles with maximum details .The core i7 6th Gen processor is not that fast at the basic level but it does provide a performance boost and is more efficient and cooler the battery unit consists of a 48 watt hour battery which is not that satisfying, but you can easily remove the battery as it is not included within the frame. The laptop can withstand a light usage for 5 hours with screen brightness set around 130 nits.

The laptop runs on a 64bit OS structure with Windows 10 preinstalled.It also has an Optical disk drive, a 1.2 Mega Pixel fixed focus HD camera.
The Laptop weighs around 2.5 Kg and will give the usuall bulky feel which is common in the gaming genre.The company also provides you an Onsite Global Warranty of 2 years which is very comforting for the buyers.
